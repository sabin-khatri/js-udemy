Our Story

Welcome to Chiya Ghar, where every cup tells a story. Born from a deep love for authentic Nepali tea culture, we started as a small dream to bring the warmth and comfort of traditional chiya to our community.

We meticulously source the finest tea leaves and spices, brewing each cup with passion and care. Our cozy space is designed to be your 'ghar' (home) away from home – a place to relax, connect, and savor the simple joy of a perfect cup of tea.

Join us and experience the heart of Nepal, one sip at a time.
